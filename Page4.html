Top Cricket Records

Highest Individual Score in ODIs

The record for the highest individual score in an ODI match is held by Rohit Sharma, with a score of 264 runs against Sri Lanka in 2014 at the Eden Gardens, Kolkata.

Most Wickets in ODIs

The record for most wickets in ODI matches is held by Muttiah Muralitharan from Sri Lanka, with 534 wickets in 350 matches.

Fastest Century in ODIs

AB de Villiers holds the record for the fastest century in ODIs, achieving it in just 31 balls against the West Indies in 2015.

Highest Team Score in an ODI

South Africa holds the record for the highest team score in ODIs, with 439/2 against West Indies in 2015.

Top Players

  • Top Batsman: Virat Kohli - Known for his consistency, he has scored over 12,000 runs in ODIs.
  • Top Bowler: Shane Warne - A legendary leg-spinner from Australia with over 700 wickets in Tests.
  • Best All-Rounder: Jacques Kallis - With more than 10,000 runs and 290 wickets in both formats, Kallis was one of the best all-rounders in cricket history.
