Our Star Players

Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is an elegant opening batsman known for his record-breaking centuries in limited overs cricket. He holds the record for the highest individual score in a One-Day International (ODI), with 264 runs against Sri Lanka.

Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli, the former captain of India, is known for his aggressive batting and exceptional run-scoring ability. He has broken numerous records and is considered one of the best modern-day batsmen.

Jasprit Bumrah

Jasprit Bumrah is a right-arm fast bowler who has become India's premier bowler, especially in death overs. His yorkers and pace make him a deadly asset in limited-overs cricket.
